Hungry Siren

Scientific Name:

Siren

Discovered by / at:

Juan Campos, 04:31

Family:

Chondrichthyes, the same as sharks

Habitat:

Swimming across the 7 seas looking for more food

Appearance:

The creature has a more terryfing face due to the lack of human like features. It has a sharp tail that seems to be able to cut fish. It also has a purple color with mennacing black eyes.

Key Characteristics:

  • Stays in the ocean
  • Eats fish
  • Swims fast

What we know so far about Siren? The siren is a sea creature capable of being found any where that there is a plentiful amount of fish. It is a creature that spends its time alone avoiding other creatures of the same kind. You will never find more than one within the same area. These creatures are known to be self reproducing and leaving their children when they are very young. This is the only Siren we have sucessfully been able to track due to the species avoiding humans.
Guidelines for human interaction with this creature We have discovered that we are able to control the Siren if we use our arrow keys when we are close to the creature.
